Wirecut Bricks

The wirecut bricks are the bricks manufacture in factory other than in yards like the normal bricks. The face finishing is much superior than the normal bricks. For the same reason, this bricks is used for exposed masonry.

Usage

Wirecut bricks are used for exposed or non plastered construction of interior or exterior walls.



By using a single purling ( support) you can fix both roof tile as well as ceiling tiles, which will give you aesthetically appealing functional roof. You can also different underlay sheets ( See Roofing Components For Details ) in between the roof tiles and ceiling tiles to make the roof 100% waterproof. You can choose either Rain Shield,Radiant Barrier or Veltision sheet to check the leakage for ever.

Specification

Available Size: 9 x 4 x 3 (Inches)
Requirement Per Sft: 5.5 Nos
