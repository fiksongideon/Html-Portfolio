TERRACOTTA WALL CLADDING

Wall Claddings are a smart alternative for exposed brick masonry or and more realistic or rugged alternative for wall tiles. As wire cut-bricks is a construction material the final finishing is highly depended on how carefully the other construction works are happening in the site and how good the brick is protected during other masonry works going on the construction site.

The wall cladding is a finishing material so the surface finishing is far better than the bricks. This can be used vertically or horizontally as sowed in the photo. And this also provide and extra insulation to the walls so that an added advantage of energy saving.
